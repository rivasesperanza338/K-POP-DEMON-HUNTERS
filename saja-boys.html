The Saja Boys

Demon Idols in Disguise

The Saja Boys (Korean: 사자 보이즈) are the secondary antagonists in K‑Pop Demon Hunters. The five‑member boy group — Mystery, Abby, Jinu, Baby, and Romance — secretly operate as demons. Their purpose: weaken Huntrix’s fanbase and destroy the Honmoon, enabling the demon Gwi‑Ma to rise into the human world.

Mystery

The enigmatic and calculating leader of the Saja Boys. His charm hides a cruel, ancient power.

Abby

The vocalist whose hypnotic voice can manipulate human emotions and drain spiritual energy.

Jinu

The powerhouse fighter of the group, capable of unleashing explosive demonic force.

Baby

A deceptively innocent‑looking demon specializing in illusions and psychological tricks.

Romance

The seductive manipulator who feeds on admiration and love from unsuspecting fans.
